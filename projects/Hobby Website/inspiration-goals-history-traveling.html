Will I ever stop traveling?

I will definitely never stop traveling, it's one of my favorite things I look forward to because I get to connect with so many people. The one problem is that as I get older, the less time I have to travel but that just makes me appreciate it just that much more. I hope to one day travel all over Europe and get to explore other places I've never seen.

Traveling has always existed but the way we do it now is much more efficient. Believe me I'd rather drive a car than ride a horse for months. Not only can it be a fun time, but it can also be a way to learn more about history and the rest of the world.
