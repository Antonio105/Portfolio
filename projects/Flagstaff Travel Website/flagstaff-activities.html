1. Snowboarding

Arizona Snowbowl is one of Arizona's two ski resorts and is located only 7 miles northwest of Flagstaff on the iconic San Francisco Peaks. The ski lifts run up Mt. Humphreys, with a summit elevation of 11,500 feet. The average snowfall in the past is 260 inches, but Snowbowl has been given permission to make snow in hopes of providing a more consistent and enjoyable snow season.

2. Historic Downtown Exploration

Flagstaff's is known for its historic downtown with many iconic scenes from movies and
TV shows. There are also many information centers that are packed with recourses
about Flagstaff, Route 66, and other places in Arizona that are only a drive away from
Flagstaff.

3. Visiting Parks

A short walk from the visitors center will bring you to Wheeler Park, an open green space with plenty of activities. This is one of many parks all around Flagstaff in which many events like community gatherings take place.
