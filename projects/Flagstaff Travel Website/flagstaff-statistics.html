Flagstaff Activities Schools Statistics

1. Population

Flagstaff has a population of around 69,903 people and is part of Coconino County. Many young professionals live in Flagstaff and residents tend to have moderate political views. The population is growing at a slow rate and job opportunities are also increasing at a steady rate. The diversity of ethnic groups has also seen a drastic change in recent years.

2. Real Estate

The median home value in Flagstaff is about $296,700, and most people rent their homes.
But this is do to the fact that a large amount of people that live in Flagstaff are there for
education or job opportunities. Property values are also increasing as more people want to
move into Flagstaff, so buying a home could be a good investment.

3. Commute and Transportation

Flagstaff has a below average commute time of only about 15.7 minutes. Public transportation is also a popular way to get around town as more and more bus lines are being created. A decent amount of people also work from home, and Flagstaff is a very social community resulting in an above average rate of carpooling within the more densely populated areas.
